# Chickpea Cobb Salad with Egg, Avocado & Creamy Asiago Dressing
tags: chickpea, cobb-salad, egg, avocado, lettuce, tomato, almonds, asiago, maple, salad, vegetarian
time: 30min
serves: 4

## Ingredients
- 2 cans chickpeas
- 8 free range brown eggs
- 2 heads lettuce
- 2 avocados
- 300 g tomatoes
- 80 g green onion
- 2/3 cup almonds
- 2/3 cup asiago cheese
- 2 tbsp lemon juice
- 2 tsp smoked paprika
- oil
- salt and pepper to taste

Maple blend (10 tsp total):
- 4 tsp maple syrup
- 4 tsp GF soy sauce
- 2 tsp rice vinegar

Dressing blend (12 tbsp total):
- 6 tbsp mayonnaise
- 4 tbsp sour cream
- 4 tsp white wine vinegar
- 2 tsp dijon mustard

Spice blend (4 tsp total):
- 2 tsp parsley
- 1 tsp granulated garlic
- 1 tsp onion powder

## Steps

### 1. Prepare Soft Boiled Eggs
- Bring 2 litres water to a boil in a pot.
- Carefully place eggs in using a slotted spoon; set timer for 7 minutes.
- Fill a large bowl with ice cold water.
- Transfer eggs to ice bath for 2–3 minutes until cooled.
- Peel and slice in half. (Or cook to preference — a less runny yolk, boil longer.)

### 2. Prepare Ingredients
- Remove leaves from lettuce stems; roughly chop into 1-inch pieces.
- Slice tomatoes in half.
- Thinly slice green onion.
- Cut avocados in half, remove pits, slice into 1/4 inch slices and scoop flesh out with a large spoon.

### 3. Prepare Almonds
- Heat a large non-stick skillet over medium heat.
- Add almonds; cook 3–4 minutes, stirring occasionally, until golden.
- Add maple blend and stir 30 seconds until liquid evaporates.
- Pour into a small bowl and toss with 1/2 tsp smoked paprika to taste.
- Clean skillet with hot water and pat dry.

### 4. Prepare Chickpeas
- Drain chickpeas in a fine mesh sieve; pat dry with paper towel.
- Heat 4 tbsp oil in the same skillet over medium-high.
- Add chickpeas; cook 8–10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until lightly crisped and golden.

### 5. Prepare Asiago Dressing
- In a bowl, stir together dressing blend, asiago cheese, and 1 tsp spice blend.
- Season to taste with lemon juice, salt, and pepper.

### 6. Finish + Assemble
- When chickpeas are cooked, stir in remaining spice blend; season to taste with remaining smoked paprika, salt, and pepper.
- Arrange lettuce across a large platter or two bowls.
- Place tomatoes, avocado, green onion, eggs, almonds, and chickpeas in sections on top.
- Serve with asiago dressing on the side. Enjoy!
