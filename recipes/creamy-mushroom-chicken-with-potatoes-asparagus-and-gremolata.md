# Creamy Mushroom Chicken with Potatoes, Asparagus & Gremolata
tags: chicken, mushroom, asparagus, potato, cream, lemon, parsley, gremolata, garlic, freshprep, roasted
time: 30min
serves: 2
source: FreshPrep

## Ingredients
- 1 package diced chicken breast
- 300 g baby potatoes
- 100 g mushrooms
- 180g asparagus
- 60g onion
- 4 tsp minced garlic
- 1/3 cup cream
- 2 tsp orange juice concentrate
- 1 tbsp white wine vinegar
- 2 tbsp almonds
- 1/4 tsp mushroom stock cube
- 2 tbsp grated parmesan cheese
- 1 tsp herb blend
- 1/4 cup fresh parsley
- 1 lemon
- olive oil
- salt and pepper to taste

## Steps

### 1. Cook Potatoes
- Turn oven to 425°F (218°C). Line a baking sheet with foil.
- Cut potatoes into 1/2 inch pieces and toss on baking sheet with 2 tbsp olive oil.
- Season to taste with salt and pepper. Roast for 10 minutes.

### 2. Prepare Ingredients
- Trim 2 inches off the ends of asparagus and discard.
- Zest and cut lemon in half. Pick and finely chop parsley.
- Cut mushrooms into 1/4 inch slices.
- Drain and pat dry chicken.

### 3. Prepare Gremolata
- In a bowl with parsley, combine 1¼ tbsp olive oil, orange juice concentrate, almonds, one-third of the garlic, and both lemon zest and lemon juice to taste.
- Season to taste with salt and red pepper flakes.

### 4. Cook Asparagus
- Once Step 1 timer goes off, toss asparagus with 1/2 tbsp olive oil on the baking sheet and season to taste with salt and pepper.
- Return to oven for 12–15 minutes, or until vegetables are golden and tender.

### 5. Cook Chicken + Mushroom Sauce
- Heat 1½ tbsp olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high.
- Once hot, add chicken and cook for 2 minutes on one side. Flip chicken and push to one side.
- Add mushrooms and onion. Cook for 2–4 minutes, stirring occasionally, until chicken and onions are golden.
- Stir in remaining garlic and cook for 30 seconds, or until fragrant.
- Add white wine vinegar and scrape bottom of skillet. Cook for 30 seconds, or until mostly evaporated.
- Reduce heat to medium, stir in cream, 1 tsp herb blend, 1/2 cup water, crumbled stock cube and parmesan cheese.
- Add remaining herb blend to taste.
- Cook for 2–4 minutes until chicken reaches internal temperature of 165°F (74°C) and sauce has thickened.
- Season to taste with pepper.

### 6. Serve
- Divide potatoes, asparagus and chicken onto serving plates.
- Top chicken with mushroom sauce.
- Garnish with gremolata.
