# Feta & Corn Fritters with Chipotle Mayo & Roasted Broccoli
tags: feta, corn, fritters, broccoli, chipotle, mayo, lime, cilantro, scallion, garlic, vegetarian, pan-fried
time: 40min
serves: 4
source: FreshPrep

## Ingredients
- 800 g broccoli
- 3 cups corn kernels
- 1 cup feta cheese
- 2 frees range egg
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 4 tbsp buttermilk
- 2 tsp baking powder
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 2 tsp spice blend (1/2 tsp smoked paprika, 1/2 tsp roasted garlic powder)
- 2 tsp ground chipotle pepper
- 4 tbsp lime juice
- 4 tsp minced garlic
- 16 g cilantro
- 80 g green onion
- olive oil
- salt and pepper to taste

## Steps

### 1. Prepare Ingredients
- Turn oven to 425°F (218°C). Line a baking sheet with foil.
- Pick and chop cilantro. Thinly slice scallions.

### 2. Cook Broccoli
- Toss broccoli on baking sheet with 2 tbsp oil and garlic.
- Bake for 15–17 minutes, or until broccoli is crisp and tender.

### 3. Prepare Fritters
- In a large bowl, whisk together buttermilk, eggs, baking powder and 1/2 tsp salt.
- Stir in flour, corn, crumbled feta and scallions. Mix well.
- Form mixture into 16 balls. Season to taste with salt. Wash hands.

### 4. Cook Fritters
- Line a plate with paper towel.
- Heat 2 tbsp oil in a large non-stick skillet over medium-high.
- Carefully add fritter balls and reduce heat to medium. Cook for 3–4 minutes, then flip.
- Using a spatula, press down lightly to flatten into 1-inch thick patties.
- Cook for another 3–4 minutes until golden and crispy on the outside and cooked through the centre.
- Transfer to lined plate to drain. Add additional oil between batches as needed.

### 5. Prepare Chipotle Mayo
- In a small bowl, combine mayonnaise, chipotle spice blend and cilantro.
- Season with salt, half of the lime juice and chipotle pepper to taste.

### 6. Serve
- Divide fritters onto plates.
- Add roasted broccoli and chipotle mayo on the side.
- Add any remaining lime juice over broccoli if desired.
- Enjoy!
