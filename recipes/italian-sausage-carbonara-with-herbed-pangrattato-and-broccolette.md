# Italian Sausage Carbonara with Herbed Pangrattato & Broccolette
tags: sausage, pasta, egg, broccolette, rigatoni, parmesan, italian, carbonara, baked
time: 40min
serves: 4
source: FreshPrep

## Ingredients
- 2 packages ground Italian sausage
- 280 g rigatoni
- 6 free range brown eggs
- 2 tbsp whipping cream
- 1/3 cup parmesan cheese
- 1/4 cup panko breadcrumbs
- 2 broccolette
- 100 g onion
- 1 tbsp garlic
- 8 g parsley
- 1 tsp coarse black pepper
- olive oil
- salt and pepper to taste

## Steps

### 1. Prepare Herbed Pangrattato
- Turn oven on to 425°F.
- Bring 2 litres salted water to a boil in a large pot.
- Pick and chop parsley.
- Heat 1 tbsp olive oil in a large non-stick skillet over medium-high.
- Once hot, add breadcrumbs and parsley and cook for 1–2 minutes until fragrant and golden.
- Transfer into a bowl and set skillet aside.

### 2. Prepare Egg Mixture
- Crack 2 whole eggs into a medium bowl.
- For remaining eggs, add only yolks to same bowl, discarding whites or storing for another meal.
- Add cream, parmesan, and coarse black pepper to taste and whisk until evenly combined.

### 3. Cook Pasta + Broccolette
- Once water is boiling, add pasta and cook for 8–10 minutes or until al dente.
- Reserve 1/2 cup pasta water, then drain pasta in a sieve and set aside for Step 5.
- Trim 1 inch from stems of broccolette and cut any large stems in half lengthwise.
- Line a baking sheet with foil. Combine broccolette with 2 tbsp olive oil and salt and pepper to taste.
- Bake for 14–18 minutes until tender with crispy edges.

### 4. Cook Sausage
- Heat 1 tbsp olive oil in the skillet from Step 1 over medium-high.
- Add onion and cook for 1–2 minutes until golden.
- Add sausage and garlic to skillet.
- Cook for 4–5 minutes, breaking apart sausage with a wooden spoon, until meat reaches an internal temperature of 165°F.

### 5. Finish Pasta
- Return cooked pasta to pot and remove from heat.
- Add Italian sausage and onion mixture to the pot.
- Slowly stir 1/4 cup reserved pasta water into the egg mixture, then add to pot.
- Stir until pasta is evenly coated, adding remaining pasta water 1 tbsp at a time until desired sauce consistency is reached.

### 6. Serve
- Divide pasta and broccolette into serving bowls and garnish with pangrattato.
- Enjoy!
