# Mediterranean Hummus Flatbread with Kalamata Olives, Bell Peppers & Feta
tags: hummus, flatbread, bell-pepper, tomato, feta, kalamata-olives, basil, pizza-dough, balsamic, za'atar, cornmeal, mediterranean, vegetarian, baked
serves: 4

> Makes 2 flatbreads — work in batches or use two baking sheets simultaneously.

## Ingredients
- 2 pkg pizza dough
- approx. 1 1/2 cups housemade tomato basil hummus
- 400 g bell pepper
- 300 g tomatoes
- 2/3 cup kalamata olives
- 2/3 cup feta
- 16 g basil
- 4 tbsp balsamic reduction
- 4 tbsp flour
- 4 tsp cornmeal
- 2 tsp za'atar
- olive oil
- salt and pepper to taste

## Steps

### 1. Cook Dough
- Turn broiler to 450°F with a rack in the centre.
- Brush each baking sheet with 1 tbsp olive oil and sprinkle 2 tsp cornmeal over top.
- Flour countertop and roll each dough ball into a large oval approximately 1/4 inch thick.
- Transfer to baking sheets and poke holes all over with a fork.
- Bake 4–6 minutes until starting to bubble. Flip each crust, return to oven for 7–11 minutes until light golden. Set aside.
- Tip: no rolling pin? A wine bottle or water glass works great.

### 2. Prepare + Cook Peppers
- Cut peppers into 1/4 inch rings.
- Heat 2 tbsp olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high. Add peppers and tomatoes; cook 2–3 minutes until golden.
- Reduce to medium and cook 4–6 more minutes until tender and tomatoes begin to burst.
- Season with salt and pepper.

### 3. Finish Flatbread + Serve
- Tear basil into 1-inch pieces.
- Spread hummus evenly over each crust edge to edge.
- Top with peppers, tomatoes, olives, crumbled feta, and balsamic reduction.
- Sprinkle za'atar to taste, garnish with basil, slice, and enjoy!
