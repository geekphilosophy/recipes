# Mexican Veggie Tortilla Lasagna with Mushroom Crumble Chili & Avocado
tags: vegetarian, mushroom, tortilla, black-beans, corn, avocado, mexican, lasagna, baked, cheese
time: 35min
serves: 4
source: FreshPrep

## Ingredients
- 1 package mushroom crumble
- 1 can black beans
- 3 tortillas
- 190 g carrot and onion
- 3/4 cup corn
- 1 avocado
- 1½ cups tomato sauce
- 1 tbsp tomato paste
- 1 tbsp garlic
- 1 cup cheese blend (aged cheddar, monterey jack, mozzarella)
- 2/3 cup sour cream
- 4½ tsp spice blend (1 tsp paprika, 1 tsp cumin, 1 tsp chili powder, 1 tsp oregano, ½ tsp coriander)
- 1 tsp ground ancho powder
- 1 tbsp chipotle pepper in adobo sauce (spicy — use to taste)
- 30 g green onion
- 1 lime
- olive oil
- salt and pepper to taste

## Steps

### 1. Cook Chili
- Drain and rinse black beans in a sieve.
- Heat 2 tbsp olive oil in a large pot over medium.
- Once hot, add carrot, onion, and corn and cook for 3–4 minutes until tender.
- Add ancho powder, chipotle in adobo to taste, tomato paste, garlic, and spice blend. Cook for 1–2 minutes until fragrant.
- Add tomato sauce, mushroom crumble, black beans, and 1/2 cup water. Bring to a boil.
- Reduce heat to medium and cook on a low boil for 5–7 minutes until slightly thickened.
- Taste and adjust seasoning with salt and pepper.

### 2. Prepare Ingredients
- Meanwhile, turn broiler on high to 500°F with a rack 6 inches from the top.
- Cut avocado in half, remove pit, dice into 1/2 inch pieces and scoop flesh out with a large spoon.
- Cut lime into quarters.
- Thinly slice green onion and add half of it to the chili.

### 3. Begin Lasagna
- Heat a large oven-proof skillet on low.
- Add one-quarter of chili, then top with one tortilla.
- Repeat until all tortillas are used, forming 3 layers.
- Top with remaining chili and cheese blend.

### 4. Finish Lasagna
- Place skillet in oven and broil for 2–4 minutes until cheese is golden and bubbling.
- Monitor closely to prevent burning.

### 5. Serve
- Garnish with avocado and remaining green onion.
- Serve family-style with sour cream and lime wedges on the side.
- Enjoy!
