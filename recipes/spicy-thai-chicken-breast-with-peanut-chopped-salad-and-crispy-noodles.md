# Spicy Thai Chicken Breast with Peanut Chopped Salad & Crispy Noodles
tags: chicken, thai, peanut, noodles, cabbage, carrot, spinach, basil, lime, soy, sesame, lemongrass, easy
time: 30min
serves: 4
source: FreshPrep

## Ingredients
- 2 packages chicken breast
- 400 g shredded cabbage
- 130 g shredded carrot
- 80 g red onion
- 120 g spinach
- 16 g fresh basil
- 2 limes
- 30 g steam-fried noodles
- 6 tbsp roasted peanuts
- 4 tbsp peanut butter
- 2 tbsp lemongrass
- 2 tbsp garlic
- 6 tbsp soy blend (1 tbsp lime juice, 1 tbsp GF soy sauce, 1 tbsp brown sugar)
- 2 tbsp fish sauce
- 2 tbsp sesame oil
- olive oil
- salt and pepper to taste

## Steps

### 1. Prepare Ingredients
- Pick and roughly chop basil.
- Zest and cut lime in half.

### 2. Prepare Dressing
- In a large bowl, whisk together peanut butter, sesame oil, soy sauce, fish sauce to taste and half of the garlic until smooth.
- Set aside.

### 3. Prepare Glaze + Cook Chicken
- In a small bowl, whisk together lemongrass, remaining garlic, lime zest and lime juice to taste.
- Drain chicken and pat dry with paper towel. Season with salt and pepper.
- Heat 1 tbsp oil in a large non-stick skillet over medium.
- Add chicken and cook for 6–8 minutes per side until golden.

### 4. Glaze Chicken
- Turn heat to low and wait 1 minute for skillet to cool slightly.
- Add prepared glaze to skillet and cook for 1–2 minutes until thickened and chicken reaches internal temperature of 165°F (74°C).
- Remove from skillet and let rest for 2–3 minutes.

### 5. Finish + Serve
- To the large bowl with dressing, add cabbage, carrot, onion, spinach, basil and steam-fried noodles.
- Toss to coat with dressing and divide into serving bowls.
- Slice chicken into 1/3 inch thick slices and place on top of salad.
- Garnish with peanuts. Enjoy!
