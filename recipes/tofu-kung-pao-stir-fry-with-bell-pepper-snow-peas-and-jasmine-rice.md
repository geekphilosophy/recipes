# Tofu Kung Pao Stir Fry with Bell Pepper, Snow Peas & Jasmine Rice
tags: tofu, stir-fry, kung-pao, jasmine-rice, bell-pepper, snow-peas, peanuts, sesame, ginger, garlic, soy-sauce, asian, chinese, vegetarian
time: 25min
serves: 4

## Ingredients
- 2 pkg tofu
- 1 1/2 cups jasmine rice
- 250 g bell peppers
- 250 g snow peas
- 180 g onion
- 70 g green onion
- 2/3 cup roasted peanuts
- 7 tsp sesame seeds
- 2 tbsp cornstarch
- 8 tsp garlic
- 2 tbsp ginger
- approx. 4 tbsp brown sugar
- 2 tbsp sesame oil
- oil
- salt and pepper to taste

Sauce blend (approx. 2/3 cup total):
- 6 tbsp GF soy sauce
- 6 tbsp sherry cooking wine
- 3 tsp rice vinegar

## Steps

### 1. Cook Rice
- Rinse and drain rice in a fine mesh sieve.
- Add to a pot with 2 1/2 cups salted water and bring to a boil.
- Reduce heat to low, cover, and cook 15–18 minutes until grains are tender.

### 2. Prepare Ingredients
- Drain tofu and pat dry with paper towel. Dice into 3/4 inch cubes.
- Cut bell peppers in half, remove stem and seeds. Dice into 1-inch pieces.
- Trim ends from snow peas.
- Thinly slice green onion.

### 3. Prepare Sauce
- Whisk 2/3 cup cold water with cornstarch until dissolved.
- Whisk in sauce blend, garlic, ginger, brown sugar, and sesame oil.

### 4. Cook Stir-Fry
- Heat 4 tbsp oil in a large non-stick skillet over medium-high.
- Add tofu; cook 4–5 minutes, turning occasionally, until all sides are golden.
- Add bell peppers, snow peas, and onion; cook 2–3 minutes until tender.
- Reduce to medium-low; add sauce, peanuts, and half the green onion.
- Cook 2–3 minutes until sauce thickens.

### 5. Finish + Serve
- Stir sesame seeds into rice.
- Divide rice between bowls and top with stir-fry.
- Garnish with remaining green onion. Enjoy!
